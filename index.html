Delicious Pancakes

A quick and easy recipe for fluffy, golden pancakes.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
Instructions
  1. In a bowl, mix flour and sugar together.
  2. Add milk and egg; whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour in batter and cook until golden brown on both sides.
